About

I’m a Computer Science: Game Design student at UC Santa Cruz with a strong interest in both game design and the business side of games, especially marketing and player research.

Most of my experience comes from working on team-based game projects, where I’ve taken on a mix of roles across design, UI, and implementation. I enjoy figuring out how different parts of a game come together, and I’ve gotten a lot of hands-on experience working with teammates, organizing playtests, and iterating based on feedback.

On my current project, MageTender, I originally pitched the idea and now help out across design, marketing, and team organization as we work toward releasing the game on Steam. That’s also pushed me to learn more about how games are presented and shared, including picking up digital marketing and thinking more about player-facing communication.

I’m especially interested in roles where I can work closely with teams, understand player behavior, and help shape both the experience of a game and how it reaches its audience!
