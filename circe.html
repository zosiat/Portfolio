About

Beware of Circe is a turn-based puzzle game inspired by Greek mythology, where players use magical potions to transform characters into different animals with unique movement patterns and abilities.

Players navigate levels by transforming NPCs into pigs, parrots, crabs, or turtles, each affecting how the environment behaves and unlocking new ways to get around obstacles. All characters move only when the player moves, meaning the game is a grid-based, turn-driven puzzle.

Gameplay

I worked on this project as part of a team of four, contributing to both NPC design as well as implementation. I developed the toolbar to make it clear to players what potions are available on each level of the game.

I also contributed to level design and helped define gameplay elements, including designing creature behaviors such as the parrot and turtle, which added new movement patterns and puzzle solutions.

Takeaways

This project was my first time working extensively in Unity, and I built a strong foundation in the engine throughout development. I contributed across multiple areas, including designing and implementing three puzzle levels, introducing mechanics like water obstacles, and creating the title screen UI and artwork.

With such a small team, it was important that we all had a broad skillset and the ability to fill in where needed. This experience helped me develop a more technical understanding of game systems overall!
